Fee Structure

Choose between a fixed fee or retainer structure for flexible, transparent pricing options

Fixed Fee

Sometimes you just need one thing or on an as needed basis for your business to support your already solid HR processes. For a fixed fee you will be provided with a customized tool, document, or process for your business. This could include areas such as training, organizational restructuring and design, employer branding, compensation and benefits strategies, employee engagement initiatives, recruitment, or strategic planning sessions.

Retainer

  • Each month, we set aside a specific number of hours just for you, giving you priority access to The Consultant. This way, we can tackle your issues and projects quickly and efficiently.
